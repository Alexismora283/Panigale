About the Ducati Panigale V4S

The Ducati Panigale V4S is a high-performance sportbike that represents the pinnacle of Ducati engineering. With a 1,103 cc V4 engine, this motorcycle produces an incredible 214 horsepower, providing an exhilarating riding experience. The Panigale V4S features top-tier electronics, including a six-axis IMU, cornering ABS, and Ducati's Slide Control, all designed to offer unparalleled performance on both the track and the road.
